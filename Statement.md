### 1\. Problem Statement



Fake banking APKs are becoming a major threat, as they mimic real banking apps to steal sensitive data like passwords, OTPs, and financial details. Users often cannot verify the authenticity of an APK before installing it. There is a need for a system that detects fake or harmful banking APKs by analyzing permissions, signatures, and suspicious behavior.





---



### 2\. Scope of the Project



Analyze uploaded APK files for authenticity.



Check permissions, metadata, package name, and certificate signatures.



Detect suspicious API calls and malicious patterns.



Provide a safety score and classify the APK as Safe, Suspicious, or Fake.



Offer a simple, user-friendly interface for quick analysis.







---



### 3\. Target Users



General mobile users installing banking apps.



Students and researchers studying Android security.



Developers verifying APK integrity.



Cybersecurity analysts checking for malicious apps.







---



### 4\. High-Level Features



APK upload and automated analysis



Permission and metadata inspection



Signature verification



Suspicious behavior detection



Risk scoring and final classification



Simple dashboard for analysis results
